All time best movies according to Nick.

My top 3 movies of all-time:

Bill and Teds Excellent Adventure

Historical fiction at its finest with a young Keanu Reeves.

Full Metal Jacket

A great Vietnam war film with an amazingly accurate depiction of Marine Corps bootcamp.

Born on The 4th of July

I really like this movie because it shows the horrors of war and military life and attempts to de-romantize the war.
